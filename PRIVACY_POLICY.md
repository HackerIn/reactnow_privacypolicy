# Privacy Policy for Quick Notes

**Last updated: October 23, 2025**

## Overview
Quick Notes is a simple, local note-taking application. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection
Quick Notes does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All notes are stored locally on your device only.

## Data Storage
- All notes are stored locally on your device
- No data is sent to external servers
- No user accounts are required
- No analytics or tracking tools are used

## Third-Party Services
Quick Notes does not use any third-party services or SDK that collect user data.

## Data Security
Your notes remain on your device and are protected by your device's security features.

## Children's Privacy
Quick Notes does not collect any information from children or anyone else.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app listing.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the Google Play Store listing.
