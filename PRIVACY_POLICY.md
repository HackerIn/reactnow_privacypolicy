# ShootX Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: May 24, 2026**
**Last Updated: May 24, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how ShootX ("ShootX," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, stores, and protects information when you use the ShootX mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

ShootX is a social video networking and meetup application. Because our Services may include user profiles, video, messaging, location-based discovery, and adult-oriented social interaction, they are intended only for users who are at least 18 years old.

By using the Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the Services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information you provide directly, including:

- **Account information:** phone number, email address, username, password or authentication information, user ID, age or age confirmation, gender, and other registration details.
- **Profile information:** photos, videos, biography, interests, preferences, and other information you choose to add to your profile.
- **User content:** videos, photos, audio, comments, messages, reports, appeals, and other content you upload, send, or share through the Services.
- **Sensitive information:** information that may be considered sensitive in some jurisdictions, such as sexual orientation, relationship preferences, precise location, images, video, biometric-related information, or age-verification media. We process sensitive information only where permitted by law and for the purposes described in this Policy.
- **Purchase information:** purchase history and subscription status. Payment card details are handled by payment processors or app stores and are not stored by ShootX.
- **Support and safety information:** communications with us, safety reports, moderation appeals, and information you provide to verify or protect your account.

### 1.2 Age Verification Information

To help comply with laws that require us to prevent minors from accessing the Services, we may process age-verification information, including:

- your response confirming whether you are over 18;
- a short 2-3 second video of the back of your hand, captured with an on-screen liveness prompt;
- frames extracted from that video, such as the first, middle, and last frame;
- if the hand check is inconclusive or does not pass, a selfie image or video for additional age review;
- appeal information you choose to submit if automated checks remain inconclusive or indicate that you may be under 18;
- verification metadata, such as user ID, check ID, capture prompt, timestamps, verification type, result, model/version, confidence fields, and reason codes.

We use this information only to assess whether you are likely to be at least 18, to prevent unauthorized access by minors, to handle appeals, to improve safety and reliability of the age-verification process, and to comply with legal obligations. We do not use hand or selfie age-verification media for advertising, user ranking, profile recommendations, behavioral profiling, or unrelated analytics.

Age verification is not intended to verify your full legal identity unless we clearly tell you that an additional identity or document-based appeal method is being offered and you choose to provide that information.

### 1.3 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Services, we may automatically collect:

- **Device information:** device type, operating system, app version, device identifiers, mobile network information, browser information, and IP address.
- **Location information:** with your permission, precise GPS location and approximate location. We use location to show nearby users, content, groups, and distance-based features. If you disable precise location, some features may not work or may rely on approximate location.
- **Usage data:** app screens viewed, features used, searches, interactions, timestamps, session duration, and other product interaction data.
- **Diagnostics:** crash logs, performance data, error logs, and security logs.
- **Cookies and similar technologies:** website cookies, analytics tags, and similar technologies used to maintain and improve the Services.

### 1.4 Information From Third Parties

We may receive information from:

- app stores and payment providers;
- authentication, hosting, analytics, safety, fraud prevention, and customer support providers;
- other users who interact with you, report content, invite you, or tag you;
- legal, safety, or law-enforcement sources where permitted or required by law.

## 5. Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law.

- **Age-verification media:** hand videos, extracted frames, and selfie media are retained only as long as necessary to complete the check, handle an appeal, maintain security, or comply with legal obligations. Our current technical design is to automatically expire age-verification uploads after a short retention period, currently about 7 days, unless a longer period is needed for an appeal, safety incident, legal obligation, or dispute.
- **Age-verification metadata:** verification result records and audit metadata may be retained longer than raw media, currently about 30 days in ordinary cases, unless a longer period is required for legal, safety, appeal, audit, or dispute purposes.
- **Account and profile data:** retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after deletion where needed for backup, legal, safety, fraud-prevention, dispute, or compliance purposes.
- **Safety reports and moderation records:** retained as needed to protect users, enforce policies, respond to legal obligations, and prevent repeat abuse.

When information is no longer needed, we delete, de-identify, or aggregate it in accordance with applicable law and our technical capabilities.
